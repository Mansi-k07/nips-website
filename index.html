About Our School

Our Introduction

New Ideal Public School is a premier educational institution located at Huddapar, Maner, Patna, Bihar. Following the CBSE pattern of education, we provide holistic development from Nursery through Class 8. Our dedicated faculty, modern infrastructure, and child-centred pedagogy make us the preferred choice of families across the region.

We believe every child carries infinite potential. Our environment is designed to spark curiosity, encourage creativity, and instill lifelong values in every learner who walks through our doors.

Our Vision

To be a centre of academic excellence that nurtures compassionate, confident, and globally aware citizens ready to lead tomorrow's world.

Our Mission

  • Deliver quality education through innovative teaching methods
  • Develop critical thinking and creative problem-solving skills
  • Foster a culture of respect, integrity, and inclusivity
  • Partner with parents for wholesome child development

Director's Message

"Welcome to New Ideal Public School — a place where young minds blossom and futures are shaped with care. At NIPS, we hold the firm belief that education is not merely the transfer of knowledge but the ignition of a child's inner light.

Our school stands on the twin pillars of academic rigour and moral values. Every teacher here is a mentor who inspires beyond the textbook, and every classroom is a sanctuary where questions are celebrated. We remain committed to providing every child with an environment that is safe, stimulating, and full of opportunity.

Together, with your trust and our dedication, we will help your child reach their fullest potential."
